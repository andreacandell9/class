Fabada Asturiana

Fabada Asturiana is a hearty spanish bean stew from Asturias, made with large white beans, chorizo, morcilla and pork.

Ingredients

  • Cannelini Beans
  • Morcilla
  • Bacon
  • Spanish Chorizo
  • 6 cups of water

Steps

  • In a large pot add the cubed bacon, on medium high heat, let the fat melt, then add the spanish chorizo.
  • Next drain the 4 cans of cannelini beans and add to the large pot of, following 6 cups of water. Bring it to a boil and switch to simmer.
  • Add the morcilla and let cook for about 15 minutes.
  • Once the 15 minutes have passed take the morcilla and chorizo out and cut, once cut, add back to the stew and let it simmer for about 20 minutes until the stew thickens.
